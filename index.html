User Agreement & Privacy Policy

Please review the terms of service for the DentAssist Clinic Management System:

  • 1. Information Collection We collect personal data (Name, DOB, Phone, Email) and medical records exclusively for dental treatment and scheduling purposes.
  • 2. Data Privacy & Security Your information is encrypted and stored securely. Only authorized clinic staff and your assigned dentist have access to your clinical notes.
  • 3. Cancellation Policy Patients are required to notify the clinic at least 24 hours before their scheduled time if they wish to cancel or reschedule.
  • 4. Reward Points System Loyalty points are earned after each completed visit. These points are non-transferable and can only be redeemed for discounts or free services within this clinic.
  • 5. Medical Accuracy You are responsible for providing accurate medical history, including allergies and current medications, to ensure safe treatment.
  • 6. Liability DentAssist is a management platform. Clinical outcomes are the responsibility of the licensed dental professional performing the treatment.
